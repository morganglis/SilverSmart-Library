About Us

What is the SilverSmart Library?

SilverSmart Library was originally founded in 19XX by a handful of passionate individuals that had in common the desire to promote and foster education and
learning. Keeping that spirit in mind, we are proud to make available to the public an extensive collection of materials on a wide variety of topics.



What can I find in the Library?

SilverSmart Library hosts a wide variety of materials including but not limited to:

  • Novels
  • Magazines
  • Scholarly Articles
  • DVDs
  • ...and more!

May I rent items from the Library?

All patrons with a valid library ID card are able to rent up to 20 items from the library at a time. To obtain a library card, you may either:

  • Sign up to receive a valid card online.
  • Visit us in-person to register for a valid card.
