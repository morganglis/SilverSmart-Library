Welcome to the SilverSmart Library Foundation

About Us

Libraries have been integral to our community for generations. Since its inception in 2023, the SilverSmart Library Foundation has been a steadfast supporter of our library services, dedicated to enhancing programs for all ages while diligently growing an endowment for future generations. Private support is indispensable to our mission.

Information: A Transformative Power

Free and open access to information is transformative, particularly for those community members who may not otherwise have access to such valuable resources. It paves the way for a brighter future for the entire community we all cherish.

Libraries vs. The Internet

In an age where it's often said that the Internet has replaced the need for libraries, it's important to remember that not everyone has easy access to computers, and not all information is readily available online. The Internet complements libraries, but it can't fully replace them. The web isn't free, and some research materials can be prohibitively expensive. Libraries provide crucial access, offering a welcoming and clean environment for those in search of knowledge, as well as a space for meaningful human interaction, which is increasingly rare in an age of digital isolation. In summary, a Google search can never truly replace the library.

Accessible Knowledge for All

Knowledge should always be accessible to all, whether it's in the realms of business and finance, hobbies and crafts, consumer health, or auto repair. All of these resources can be found in the Silversmart Library, which serves as a hub for our community to gather knowledge and exchange information. We invite you to partner with us in our mission to "raise funds from the community to enhance and enrich the SilverSmart Library system." Together, we can continue to make our library a vital resource for generations to come.
