Terms of Payment for SilverSmart Library

Payment is due within a number of days determined by items rented. A late fee determined by the items rented applies after the due date. Please reference the rental invoice number during payment. Report any discrepancies within 7 days of receipt.

Accepted Payment Methods

SilverSmart Library accepts the following payment methods:

  • Cash
  • Credit
  • Debit

Payments must be made in-person upon return of rented items.
